Introduction

FoodIn is an innovative Irish company that has been transforming the way restaurants, takeaways, and retail businesses operate since its founding in 2021. In just three short years, FoodIn has grown from a start-up idea into a trusted nationwide provider of technology, branding, and support solutions. With our headquarters in Ireland and a rapidly growing client base across the country, FoodIn is committed to helping local businesses modernise, grow, and compete with confidence in today’s digital marketplace.

Our Story

FoodIn was born out of a simple observation: many local restaurants and takeaways were struggling to keep up with changing customer expectations and rising costs. Large third-party platforms offered online ordering but charged high commissions that reduced profits. Traditional POS systems worked but often required multiple vendors, printers, card machines, and software updates that created confusion and extra expenses. Business owners needed a single, reliable partner who could deliver everything under one roof — from technology to signage, from websites to real-time support.

That’s where FoodIn came in. Founded in 2021, our mission has always been to provide a smarter, more affordable alternative that empowers local businesses to run efficiently, improve customer experiences, and keep more of their hard-earned revenue.

Our Mission

FoodInn’s mission is simple: to give every restaurant, takeaway, and retailer in Ireland access to the same powerful tools big brands use, but at a fair and affordable price. We believe that technology should make life easier, not harder. By combining multiple services under one roof, we remove the stress of juggling different providers and reduce unnecessary costs.

Why Choose FoodIn?

  • One Partner, Every Solution: No more dealing with five different companies for POS, websites, and signage — FoodIn does it all.
  • Affordable and Transparent: Our pricing is designed to save money and protect profit margins.
  • Future-Ready Technology: We bring world-class digital tools to local businesses without complexity.
  • Professional Branding: From menus to digital screens, we help you look polished and consistent.
  • Nationwide Reach: Though based in Ireland, our services support businesses in every county.
  • Proven Growth: In just three years, FoodIn has successfully supported dozens of businesses and processed thousands of orders nationwide.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Always improving and introducing smarter ways to run businesses.
  • Reliability: Round-the-clock support means peace of mind.
  • Affordability: Solutions that fit local business budgets without hidden costs.
  • Partnership: We see every client as a long-term partner, not just a customer.

Looking Ahead

The future of food and retail is digital, and FoodIn is here to make sure no local business is left behind. Over the coming years, we aim to expand our services even further — adding advanced analytics, customer loyalty programs, and integrated marketing tools. By continuing to innovate, we’ll help Irish businesses compete, grow, and thrive in an increasingly competitive market.
