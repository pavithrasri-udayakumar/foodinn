POS Ordering System

Running a food business is fast-paced. Customers want quick service, staff need simple tools, and owners need control over every order and payment. FoodInn’s POS Ordering System is designed with restaurants and takeaways in mind, giving you a reliable, all-in-one solution that makes every part of your daily operations easier.

Our system is built for speed. The touchscreen interface is easy to learn, so staff can take orders confidently within minutes. Favourite items, combos, and add-ons are just a tap away, meaning fewer mistakes and faster checkout. Whether you’re serving walk-in customers, managing deliveries, or handling collections, everything works together seamlessly.

To keep your business flexible, FoodInn POS accepts both cash and card payments, including contactless, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Delivery features such as postcode recognition, driver assignment, and order tracking are built right in, so your team doesn’t need to juggle separate systems. You’ll also have the peace of mind of knowing the POS works even if your internet connection drops — once you’re back online, everything syncs automatically.

  • Fast by default. Clean, simple screens that speed up service.
  • Delivery & collection tools. manage drivers and orders in one place.
  • Cash & card. Integrated payments with tips and surcharges.
  • Controls that protect margin. Void/discount rules, staff roles, audit trail.
  • Real insights. Z-reads, VAT, item & category performance.

Hardware options available (printers, cash drawers, caller ID). We set it up, you start selling.

Website & Online Ordering

Third-party delivery apps might bring you orders, but they also take a big cut of your revenue and put your brand behind theirs. With FoodInn’s Website & Online Ordering system, you get your own branded site that belongs to you. Fair, transparent pricing — lower commission and simple costs that save you money compared to third-party apps. You keep more of your order revenue while building your own customer base.

Our online ordering platform is designed for the way people actually order today: on their phones. The mobile-first checkout is fast, simple, and built to convert. Customers can browse your full menu, customise orders, and pay with Apple Pay, Google Pay, or any major card in just a few taps. Meal deals, upsells, and add-ons appear at the right moment to increase the average basket size without slowing things down.

Even better, your website and POS system work together. When you update your menu in FoodInn, the changes instantly appear online — no duplication, no mistakes, no confusion for customers. Orders from the website flow directly into your EPOS and kitchen printer, so your team handles them just like walk-ins.

  • More margin.keep your order revenue with zero third-party commission.
  • Bigger baskets. built-in upsells, meal deals, and timed offers.
  • Findable online. SEO & Google Business done right.
  • Flexible fulfilment. Delivery & click-and-collect with slots.
  • Loyalty & coupons. Reward regulars and track performance.

Includes order scheduling, customer accounts, and automated confirmation emails.

LED Screens for Your Shopfront

Your storefront is the first thing customers notice. With FoodInn’s LED Screens, you can make that first impression bold, modern, and impossible to miss. Our high-brightness digital panels cut through daylight glare, so your menu and promotions look crisp and eye-catching at any time of day.

Digital signage gives you flexibility traditional posters can’t match.
